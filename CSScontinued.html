Drum Fun Facts!

Click the button to learn a drum fact!

The drums are loud! They are the loudest instrument and don't require electricity to play!

Drums are used in cultures all over the world and appear in styles ranging from rock and jazz to hip-hop, orchestral music, and traditional ceremonial music.

Drummers are often in high demand since solid rhythm players are harder to find than many other instrumentalists.

Studying drums builds discipline, focus, and pattern recognition through regular practice. Most importantly, drums naturally make people move, and rhythm creates an immediate physical and emotional connection with listeners.
